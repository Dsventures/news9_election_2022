The 18th state Assembly elections are scheduled to be held in Gujarat from February 10 to March 7, 2022 in seven phases to elect 403 members of the state Assembly. The results will be declared on March 10, 2022. The Yogi Adityanath-led Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) government is ruling the state since 2017.
